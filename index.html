ABOUT US
Welcome to Speak Up

Speak Up is an organisation created to reach young school students and impart the skill of Debating to them. In a world where the divide between the privileged and the underprivileged is constantly increasing at an exponential rate, Speak Up believes that it is rudimentary to learn how to get your voice heard....

We believe that everyone must know how to raise their opinion and concerns whenever needed, in a manner that is powerful and easy to understand. With the bigger vision clear in our heart and our head, we work towards a Better Bharat.

OUR FOUNDER
Mr. Ujjwal Tiwary

Ujjwal Tiwary is a pre-final year student at IIT Kharagpur. He picked up Debating as a hobby in his freshman year and has explored the field in great depth. To him, debating is just like another sport that teaches you skills that will aid you in every sphere of your life.

With an intent to impart the art of Debating to school students who may, otherwise, not have an access to it, he created Speak Up
